# Definition of Done (DoD) — IPM-INC-003 BRD Writing

Quality gates that must ALL pass for a BRD to be considered complete.

## Completeness Checks

| ID | Check | Severity |
|----|-------|----------|
| DOD-C01 | All standard BRD sections present and populated with meaningful content | Critical |
| DOD-C02 | Business objectives traceable to at least one requirement | Critical |
| DOD-C03 | Every requirement has defined, testable acceptance criteria | Critical |
| DOD-C04 | In-scope and out-of-scope items explicitly listed | Critical |
| DOD-C05 | All stakeholders documented with roles and responsibilities | High |
| DOD-C06 | All assumptions explicitly stated | High |
| DOD-C07 | Budget, time, technology, and regulatory constraints listed | High |
| DOD-C08 | External systems, teams, and deliverable dependencies identified | High |
| DOD-C09 | Risk register populated with probability, impact, and mitigation | High |
| DOD-C10 | KPIs defined, measurable, and tied to business objectives | Critical |

## Requirement Quality (per ISO/IEC/IEEE 29148)

| ID | Check | Severity |
|----|-------|----------|
| DOD-R01 | Necessary — traceable to a business need | Critical |
| DOD-R02 | Unambiguous — single interpretation, no vague language | Critical |
| DOD-R03 | Singular — one thing per requirement (no compound and/or) | High |
| DOD-R04 | Feasible — achievable within constraints | High |
| DOD-R05 | Verifiable — testable or measurable | Critical |
| DOD-R06 | Traceable — linked to source and forward to design/test | High |
| DOD-R07 | Consistent — no conflicts between requirements | Critical |
| DOD-R08 | Prioritized — MoSCoW priority assigned | High |

## Document Quality

| ID | Check | Severity |
|----|-------|----------|
| DOD-D01 | Clear, concise language accessible to non-technical stakeholders | High |
| DOD-D02 | Business objectives in SMART format | High |
| DOD-D03 | No spelling, grammar, or formatting errors | Medium |
| DOD-D04 | Version number, date, author, and change log complete | High |
| DOD-D05 | Zero TBD/TODO placeholders in final version | Critical |
