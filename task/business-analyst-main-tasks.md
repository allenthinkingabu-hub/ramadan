# Main Tasks of a Business Analyst

A Business Analyst's core responsibilities throughout the project lifecycle:

---

## 📋 Project Lifecycle Tasks

### 1. Inception Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **BA-INC-001 — Stakeholder Identification** | Identifying key stakeholders, decision-makers, and subject matter experts across business units. |
| **BA-INC-002 — Business Needs Analysis** | Conducting workshops and interviews to understand business problems, goals, and expected outcomes. |
| **BA-INC-003 — Current State Assessment (As-Is)** | Documenting existing business processes, workflows, and pain points using process mapping techniques. |
| **BA-INC-004 — Gap Analysis** | Comparing current state against desired future state to identify functional and operational gaps. |
| **BA-INC-005 — Business Case Support** | Assisting in building the business case with cost-benefit analysis, ROI projections, and value justification. |

### 2. Requirements Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **BA-REQ-001 — Requirements Elicitation** | Gathering detailed requirements through interviews, workshops, observation, document analysis, and surveys. |
| **BA-REQ-002 — Business Process Modeling (To-Be)** | Designing future-state business processes using BPMN, flowcharts, or activity diagrams. |
| **BA-REQ-003 — User Story Writing** | Translating business requirements into well-structured user stories with acceptance criteria. |
| **BA-REQ-004 — Requirements Traceability** | Maintaining a traceability matrix linking business objectives to requirements, test cases, and deliverables. |
| **BA-REQ-005 — Stakeholder Sign-Off** | Facilitating formal requirement review sessions and obtaining stakeholder approval on documented requirements. |

### 3. Sprint / Development Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **BA-DEV-001 — Requirement Clarification** | Serving as the bridge between business stakeholders and the development team for real-time queries. |
| **BA-DEV-002 — Impact Analysis** | Assessing the business and technical impact of change requests and scope adjustments. |
| **BA-DEV-003 — Data Analysis & Validation** | Analyzing data requirements, business rules, mapping data flows, and validating data migration logic. |
| **BA-DEV-004 — Process Documentation Updates** | Keeping business process documents and requirement artifacts aligned with ongoing changes. |
| **BA-DEV-005 — Demo Preparation Support** | Assisting the Product Owner in preparing sprint demos with business context and success criteria. |

### 4. QA & UAT Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **BA-QA-001 — UAT Test Case Design** | Writing UAT test scenarios and scripts based on business requirements and acceptance criteria. |
| **BA-QA-002 — UAT Facilitation** | Coordinating UAT execution with business users, tracking progress, and collecting structured feedback. |
| **BA-QA-003 — Defect Validation** | Verifying reported defects against business requirements to confirm validity and priority. |
| **BA-QA-004 — Business Readiness Assessment** | Evaluating organizational readiness including training needs, process changes, and communication plans. |

### 5. Release & Growth Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **BA-REL-001 — Training & Change Support** | Creating training materials, user guides, and conducting knowledge transfer sessions for end users. |
| **BA-REL-002 — Benefits Realization Tracking** | Monitoring post-launch KPIs to measure whether the solution delivers the expected business value. |
| **BA-REL-003 — Feedback Collection & Analysis** | Gathering user feedback and operational data to identify improvement areas for future iterations. |
| **BA-REL-004 — Lessons Learned Documentation** | Recording key insights, challenges, and best practices for organizational knowledge sharing. |
| **BA-REL-005 — Continuous Improvement Input** | Providing business-driven recommendations for product enhancements based on data and user feedback. |
