# Main Tasks of a Change Manager

A Change Manager's core responsibilities throughout the project lifecycle:

---

## 📋 Project Lifecycle Tasks

### 1. Inception Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **CM-INC-001 — Change Impact Assessment** | Evaluating the scope, scale, and complexity of organizational change introduced by the project across people, process, and technology. |
| **CM-INC-002 — Stakeholder Analysis & Mapping** | Identifying impacted stakeholder groups, assessing their change readiness, resistance risks, and influence levels. |
| **CM-INC-003 — Change Strategy Development** | Defining the overarching change management approach, methodology (ADKAR, Kotter, Prosci), and guiding principles. |
| **CM-INC-004 — Sponsorship Alignment** | Engaging executive sponsors, defining their active role in championing the change, and building a sponsorship coalition. |
| **CM-INC-005 — Change Risk Identification** | Identifying organizational change risks (adoption failure, resistance, skill gaps, cultural barriers) and mitigation plans. |

### 2. Requirements Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **CM-REQ-001 — Change Management Plan** | Creating the comprehensive change management plan covering communication, training, engagement, and resistance management. |
| **CM-REQ-002 — Communication Strategy & Plan** | Designing a multi-channel communication plan defining key messages, audiences, timing, channels, and feedback loops. |
| **CM-REQ-003 — Training Needs Analysis** | Assessing skill and knowledge gaps across impacted roles and defining training requirements, formats, and schedules. |
| **CM-REQ-004 — Change Network Establishment** | Recruiting and onboarding change champions, super users, and departmental liaisons to advocate and support the change. |
| **CM-REQ-005 — Change Readiness Baseline** | Conducting initial change readiness surveys and assessments to establish baseline metrics for tracking adoption progress. |

### 3. Sprint / Development Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **CM-DEV-001 — Stakeholder Engagement Execution** | Running ongoing stakeholder engagement activities including town halls, roadshows, demos, and feedback sessions. |
| **CM-DEV-002 — Communication Delivery** | Executing the communication plan with regular newsletters, intranet updates, video messages, and leadership talking points. |
| **CM-DEV-003 — Training Material Development** | Creating training content including e-learning modules, quick reference guides, video tutorials, and hands-on workshop curricula. |
| **CM-DEV-004 — Resistance Management** | Monitoring and addressing resistance through one-on-one coaching, focus groups, and targeted interventions for high-risk groups. |
| **CM-DEV-005 — Change Progress Tracking** | Tracking change activity completion, stakeholder sentiment, and engagement metrics against the change management plan. |

### 4. QA & UAT Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **CM-QA-001 — End-User Training Delivery** | Conducting role-based training sessions, hands-on workshops, and train-the-trainer programs ahead of go-live. |
| **CM-QA-002 — UAT Change Support** | Supporting business users during UAT by reinforcing the purpose of change, managing expectations, and gathering feedback. |
| **CM-QA-003 — Change Readiness Assessment** | Conducting go-live readiness assessments measuring training completion, stakeholder confidence, and organizational preparedness. |
| **CM-QA-004 — Go-Live Communication Preparation** | Preparing go-live announcements, support channel information, escalation contacts, and day-one user guides. |
| **CM-QA-005 — Resistance Escalation & Resolution** | Escalating persistent resistance issues to sponsors and leadership for intervention and resolution before launch. |

### 5. Release & Growth Phase
| Task | Description |
|---|---|
| **CM-REL-001 — Go-Live Change Support** | Providing on-the-ground support during go-live with floor walkers, help desks, and rapid issue escalation channels. |
| **CM-REL-002 — Adoption Monitoring & Measurement** | Tracking adoption KPIs (system usage, process compliance, proficiency levels) and comparing against success criteria. |
| **CM-REL-003 — Reinforcement Activities** | Running post-launch reinforcement through refresher training, success stories, recognition programs, and coaching. |
| **CM-REL-004 — Sustainability Planning** | Transitioning change ownership to business leaders and establishing processes to sustain the change long-term. |
| **CM-REL-005 — Change Retrospective & Lessons Learned** | Conducting change management retrospectives, documenting lessons learned, and refining the approach for future initiatives. |
